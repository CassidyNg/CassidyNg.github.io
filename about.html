About Me

Hello! My name is Cassidy Ng and I am a junior at Tappan Zee High School.

My interest in Computer Science started mainly because of my older sister, who is currently a CS major at Cornell University. She inspired me to give Computer Science a try during my freshmen year of high school, and from there, my passion grew. Throughout my high school career, I have taken several Computer Science classes and learned several languages such as Python, Java, Html, and CSS. I became a member of our Computer Science Club, Honor Society, and Robotics club. Through the projects and activities offered from both my CS classes and extracuricculars, I've been able to allow my childish creativity flow. I always enjoy seeing a program or idea run successfully. There is such satifaction and relief when I see my code do what I intended it to, without errors.

Outside of school, I am a big baker. You can find me baking for my family and friends on several occasions such as for birthdays, Christmas, Mid-Autumn Festival, and more. I love being able to treat those who are close to me. I am also a lover of fantasy and dystopian books. I can spend whole days reading a good book and searching for new ones to add to my reading list. Additionally, I always enjoy playing instruments and learning new pieces. I am a classically trained pianist of 9+ years, a cellist of 8+ years, and am currently trying to teach myself the guzheng.
