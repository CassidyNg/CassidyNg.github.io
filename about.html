About Me

Hello! My name is Cassidy Ng and I am a junior at Tappan Zee High School.

I'm going to admit it. I wasn't always a huge STEM girl; in fact, I was solely an art and music lover who had no interest in anything else. From a young age, I already had a whole bunch of sketchbooks lined up on my shelves and started my music journey by taking on piano and cello. I always thought I'd end up drawing and playing music. Who knew that I would eventually become so passionate about Computer Science and being a part of this STEM community. However, just because I changed interests it does not mean that I ran away from my childhood dreams. Through the projects and activities offered through my CS classes and extracurriculars, I've allowed by childish creativity to flow, making little fantasies of mine become a reality with code.

Academically, I am an all Honors and AP student who took 2 APs my Sophomore year, 3 APs my Junior year, and hopefully 5 Aps my Senior year. I participate in twelve extracurriculars that include tutoring, volunteering community service, performing music, participating is Computer Science related activities, programming parts of a robot, and managing a team of 51+ students. I've hosted outreach events for our school's robotics team, helped organize activities for Computer Science Club and Honor Society, and performed at our school's Multicultural Festival. As a Junior, I am also taking an online Chinese class through Virtual High School, where I do weekly vocabulary lessons, discussions, videos, and quizzes.

Outside of school, I am a big baker. You can always find me baking for my family and friends on several occasions such as for birthdays, Christmas, Mid-Autumn Festival, and more. I love being able to treat those who are close to me, and it's amazing to be able to put a smile on their faces. I am also a lover of fantasy and dystopian books. I can spend whole days reading a good book and searching for new ones to add to my reading list. Additionally, I always enjoy playing instruments and learning new pieces. I am a classically trained pianist of 9+ years, a cellist of 8+ years, and am currently trying to teach myself the guzheng.
